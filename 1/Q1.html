Q1. How many ping pong balls would it take to fill an average-sized school bus? Describe each step in your thought process.

 

Bus of Balls

Step 1

Rip out all the seats in the bus, should be fun.

Step 2

First lets figure out what we are dealing with, we have an average size school bus and hopefully an unlimited amount of standard ping pong balls. The average school bus is about 10ft in height, 8ft in width, and 40ft in length. A ping pong ball is 40mm in diameter.

Step 3

Now we do a little converting, 1 inch is equal to 25.4 millimeters, which makes a ping pong ball 1.3 inches all around. 1.3 inches into a foot is about 9 ping pong balls for each foot of the bus. Lets get some more numbers. 9 balls times 8 feet equal to 72 ping pong balls for the width, 9 balls times 10 feet equals 90 balls for the height, and 9 balls times 40 feet is equal to 360 ping pong balls for the length.

Step 4

The volume of a rectangular prism is equal to length * width *height, easy enough. We have 72 balls for the width *90 balls for the height * 360 balls for the length which comes out to 2,332,800 ping pong balls that will go into that bus, give or take a few thousand or hundred.

Step 5

Now! I don’t know about you, but we aren’t looking at the big picture here, we have a hopefully working bus and over 2 million ping pong balls, if this doesn’t scream fun I don’t know what does. *opens emergency hatch and jumps into the bus full of ping pong balls*
